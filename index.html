01 About

I'm an AI engineer specializing in multimodal perception, on-device inference, and robotic intelligence. My journey in technology began in grade 8 and grew into a focused practice in applied robotics and embodied AI, building systems deployed on real robots, not simulations.

I develop AI stacks for humanoid robots on Jetson platforms using VLM / VLA pipelines, embedding models into real-world behaviors for healthcare and industrial domains. I've built industrial vision systems for automotive manufacturing where robustness and reliability are non-negotiable.

I'm an IEEE-published researcher focused on multimodal sensing and real-time ML. Alongside this, I founded Nextgen Research Lab & Infrastructure Development Pvt. Ltd., where we're building MaaKosh, an AI-powered maternal & child healthcare ecosystem for preventive, accessible, and intelligent care from pregnancy through early childhood.
